> Plan and execute product launches that build momentum — from pre-launch hype to post-launch retention.

## What This Skill Does

**The Challenge**: Most product launches underperform because they lack structure. Teams ship without a pre-launch waitlist, announce with generic copy, and miss the 72-hour window where launch momentum converts to customers.

**The Solution**: Launch Strategy skill provides structured launch playbooks for Product Hunt, email lists, social channels, and press. Covers the 30-day pre-launch, launch day execution, and 30-day post-launch optimization phases.

## Capabilities

### 1. Launch Playbook Templates
Structured timeline for each launch type.

**Launch types**:
| Type | Channels | Timeline | Goal |
|------|---------|----------|------|
| Product Hunt | PH, Twitter, email | Launch day only | Upvotes + visibility |
| SaaS feature | Email, in-app, blog | 2-week campaign | Activation |
| Public beta | Waitlist, press | 30 days | Signups + feedback |
| Paid tier launch | Email, ads, affiliates | 60 days | Revenue |

### 2. Pre-Launch Phase (30 Days)
Build audience and anticipation before shipping.

**Pre-launch checklist**:
- [ ] Build waitlist landing page (target: 500+ emails)
- [ ] Create teaser content (3-5 behind-the-scenes posts)
- [ ] Recruit 10-20 beta users for testimonials
- [ ] Brief press and influencers (embargo until launch day)
- [ ] Prepare Product Hunt hunter and team upvoters
- [ ] Write email sequence for launch week (3 emails)
- [ ] Line up co-marketing partners

### 3. Launch Day Playbook
Hour-by-hour execution plan for launch day.

**Launch day timeline**:
```
12:01 AM — Product Hunt submission live
 6:00 AM — Email #1 to full list ("We launched!")
 9:00 AM — Twitter/LinkedIn announcement with demo video
10:00 AM — DM 50 personal connections to check it out
12:00 PM — Email #2 to segments (customers, trial, churned)
 3:00 PM — Respond to all PH comments
 6:00 PM — Update leaderboard progress tweet
```

### 4. Launch Copy Templates
Ready-to-customize copy for each channel.

**Product Hunt tagline formula**: "[Product] — [specific benefit] for [target audience]"

**Launch email subject lines**:
- "We're live on Product Hunt today 🚀" (direct)
- "The wait is over — [Product] is here" (relief)
- "You asked for [feature] — here it is" (acknowledgment)

### 5. Post-Launch Optimization (30 Days)
Convert launch visitors to retained customers.

**Post-launch metrics to track**:
- Activation rate (% who complete key action in day 1)
- Day-7 retention
- First-week revenue
- Press pickups
- Word-of-mouth referral rate

## Prerequisites

- Product functionally complete (no launch with critical bugs)
- Landing page live with analytics
- Email list with segmentation
- Social accounts active

## Best Practices

**1. Launch to a warm audience first**
Email your list before going public. Early engagement signals boost algorithmic visibility.

**2. Collect social proof before launch day**
10 real testimonials on launch day outperform any copy you write.

**3. Extend the launch window**
"We're live!" → "Last 24 hours" → "Top 5 of the week" → "Thank you" creates a 72-hour cycle.

## Common Use Cases

### Use Case 1: SaaS Product Hunt Launch
**Scenario**: Bootstrapped SaaS targeting #1 Product Hunt for the day.

**30-day plan**:
1. Week -4: Waitlist page live, begin content teasing
2. Week -3: Recruit 50 personal supporters for upvote day
3. Week -2: Brief friendly press (TechCrunch, IndieHackers)
4. Week -1: Beta user testimonial collection
5. Launch day: 6am email → 9am social → 12pm follow-up
6. Week +1: "Thank you" post + convert trial users

### Use Case 2: Feature Announcement to Existing Customers
**Scenario**: New AI feature launch to customer base.

**Email sequence**:
- Email 1 (5 days before): "Something big is coming"
- Email 2 (launch day): "Your new superpower is live"
- Email 3 (day 7): "Have you tried [feature]?" with tutorial
- In-app: Banner + tooltip on first login post-launch

## Troubleshooting

**Issue**: Product Hunt launch gets fewer than 100 upvotes
**Solution**: Audit timing (Tuesday-Thursday best). Check if supporters voted before 12pm EST. Improve tagline and thumbnail.

**Issue**: Launch spike doesn't convert to retained users
**Solution**: Check day-1 activation flow. Add onboarding email sequence. Identify where new users drop off.
